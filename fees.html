📌 Important Notes:

• Admission Fee is completely FREE for all classes. No hidden charges at the time of admission.

• Tuition fee is charged monthly. Quarterly payment is encouraged to avoid late fee.

• Exam fee is charged annually and is separate from monthly tuition fee.

• For fee payment and concession queries, please visit the school office (Mon–Sat, 8:00 AM – 4:00 PM).

• Scholarship students who qualify for the Malti Agarwal Smriti Chatravriti will receive up to 100% waiver on tuition & exam fee.
